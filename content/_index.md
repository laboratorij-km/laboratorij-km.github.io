### Laboratory for Cognitive Modeling

LKM research areas are artificial intelligence, machine learning, statistics, natural language processing, image analysis, cognitive modelling, and data mining. Research is applied to cognitive, medical, biological, financial, and other processes. We are developing, testing, and applying new approaches and algorithms for modelling from numeric, symbolic and pictorial data.
